About

Chain Guard is an intelligent, end-to-end monitoring system designed to close critical gaps in supply chain compliance. Our platform ensures on-time deliveries, maintains strict cold-chain integrity, and verifies all licensing and documentation. Through digitized workflows and real-time tracking, Chain Guard provides enhanced transparency for managers, helps vendors meet regulatory standards, and gives administrators powerful oversight tools.

Core Compliance Pillars

  • License Compliance: Tracks vehicle, driver, and permit validity with timestamped verifications.
  • Cold-Chain Compliance: Creates a unique, auditable record for every temperature log and deviation event.
  • Documentation Compliance: Assigns a unique reference to each uploaded document and its verification status.
  • Safety & Hygiene Compliance: Logs every sanitation check and inspection for a complete safety record.
